  • These are some notes about applying for visas outside your home country. I don't list all the requirements here, mainly fees.
  • Not reliable. Use this at your own risk.
  • Requirements/fees may vary depending on your nationality or the mood of the embassy worker.
  • Prices in USD.

  • Many countries want you to have a return flight and a hotel reservation to apply for visa, even if you intend to travel by land. Sometimes you might get asked to show a return flight at the airport, even if you don't need a visa. To get a "fake" flight itinerary (at your own risk):
  • Use a booking website that lets you choose bank transfer or payment at airline office as payment method (fareboom.com, KLM, Emirates, yatra.com(?)). You will get a booking number that will be valid for a few days. If you don't make a payment on time, your reservation will be cancelled automatically.
  • Business class tickets sometimes come with free cancellation.
  • US-based booking websites, e.g. expedia.com, orbitz.com (must use their US website with .com domian) offer free cancellation within 24 hours.
  • "Fake" hotel booking: many websites offer free cancellation.
