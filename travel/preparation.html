PREPARATION

Planning:
  • What are the entry requirements? (visas, permits, vaccinations...)
  • What is the current situation? (holidays, elections, violence...). Check news and travel advisory pages if not sure about safety.
  • What weather can you expect? Will you be at a high altitude?
  • If going to multiple countries, how will you cross the borders? Are land crossings open to tourists? Can you apply for visas outside your home country?
  • How will you handle money? Can you use local ATMs?
Also:
  • Write down important information (bank card numbers, passwords, plane tickets) using a secret code that only you can solve. Email it to yourself, along with scans of important documents.
  • Print out a few maps and notes about the places you want to visit.
  • Sometimes it is good to carry a printed copy of your passport.
